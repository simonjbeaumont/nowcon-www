Leading the now generation

We believe in resourcing the leaders of today's generation. Often the 0-30s is referred to as the next generation but we believe that they are the now generation and, if they are to have influence in their world tomorrow, we must invest in them today. We must invest in them now.

Joining us will be Dave Niblock, lead pastor of LIFE Church Leeds. Dave and his wife also head up ROCKNATIONS: the youth and young adults' ministry and conference at LIFE Church. Dave is a fantastic communicator and will be bringing his unique insights into reaching this generation witha relevent but uncompromising voice.
